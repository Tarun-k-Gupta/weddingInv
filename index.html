What the Heart Desired, Destiny Drew Near.

Abhilasha a heartfelt wish, a cherished desire
+
Kashish an irresistible pull, a magnetic attraction

This line feels deeply personal to us because it reflects not only the meaning hidden within our names, but also the extraordinary way in which our story unfolded. Together, these meanings tell a story of a wish finding its way to what it was always meant for.

Our journey began on Maha Shivratri, 15th February 2026 — the very first day we met. A day that celebrates the divine union of Lord Shiva and Goddess Parvati — a reminder that some bonds are guided by faith, patience, and destiny.

Ours is an arranged marriage. But from the moment our paths crossed, it felt as if the universe had been quietly working behind the scenes, bringing together two people whose stories were always meant to intervene.

The more we got to know each other, the more we realized that some things cannot be planned — they can only be discovered. Perhaps that is why our story resonates so deeply with this line. A heartfelt wish found its way home and an invisible force kept drawing it closer.
